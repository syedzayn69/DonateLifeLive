How to Donate:

 Eligibility Criteria: To donate blood, you must be in good health, weigh at least 110 pounds, and meet age requirements (usually 17 or older).

Donation Process:

Step 1: Register as a donor.

Step 2: Find a donation center near you.

Step 3: Donate and save lives!
